Workshops

Sophie regularly runs workshops for actors to help hone their audition technique, from the technicalities of a self-tape to the various different considerations of an in-person audition. She really enjoys working with actors to ensure they receive constructive feedback whilst feeling encouraged and motivated, and ensures they have the opportunity to ask any questions they may have during the session.

If you're interested in booking Sophie for a workshop, please use the contact form.

Please note, Sophie will only agree to workshops that adhere to the guidelines outlined by the Casting Directors Guild.
